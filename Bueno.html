Why
Bueno Finance?


In India, blue collar worker's life is hard, precarious and often risk prone. It is estimated that by 2021, over 15 million gig-workers will be linked to digital platforms. While the gig sector is growing, the financial services that these blue collar gig-workers can access have not kept pace. BuenoFinance is built to change that landscape and to create tailored financial products.
